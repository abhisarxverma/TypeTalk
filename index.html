Set Up TypeTalk in 3 Simple Steps And Start Talking In Calls With Your Keyboard
1

Download Safe, Free, and Light, VB-Audio Cable

Download and install the free, lightweight software called VB-Audio Virtual Cable. This tool allows your system's generated sound to act like a microphone input.

Get VB-Audio Cable (Official Site)
2

From Taskbar set Sound Output to 'Cable Input'

Go to your Taskbar and click the sound icon, then three lines, and set your output device to CABLE Input (VB-Audio Virtual Cable).

This ensures that whatever is played by your system (TypeTalk voice) will be directed into your mic input

3

Configure Your Call App

In your video call or meeting app (Zoom, Google Meet, Discord, etc.):

- Set your microphone/input to CABLE Output (VB-Audio Virtual Cable)

- Set your speaker/output to your actual headphones or speakers (e.g., "Realtek Speakers" or "Headphones")

This lets you hear others while they hear your TypeTalk-generated voice.

After Use You Can Just Switch Your System's Sound Output to your normal, like "Realtek" or "Speakers".
